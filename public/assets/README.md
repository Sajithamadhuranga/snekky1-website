# Figma asset exports

Drop your real Figma exports into this folder using the same filenames already referenced by the app:

- snekky-mascot.svg or .png
- snekky-mascot-large.svg or .png
- snekky-aboutus.svg or .png
- snekky-cowboy.svg or .png
- howtobuy-1.svg or .png
- howtobuy-2.svg or .png
- token-icon.svg or .png
- tokenomics-diagram.svg or .png
- roadmap-1.svg or .png
- roadmap-2.svg or .png
- roadmap-3.svg or .png
- metaverse-1.svg or .png
- metaverse-2.svg or .png
- icons/telegram.svg or .png
- icons/x.svg or .png
- cards/card-1.svg or .png ... card-12.svg or .png

The app will automatically try PNG/WebP/JPG fallbacks when a matching SVG is not available.
